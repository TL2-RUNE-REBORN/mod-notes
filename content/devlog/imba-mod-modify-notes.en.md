## Fun Mods
### Works by Runic Games Staff

Extra Chunky and Blanks Landmarks complement each other

> This mod and Blanks Landmarks are very similar in that they both add new levels to the game for increased variation in the world. Blanks Landmarks adds variety to the different landmarks scattered around the overworld, while Extra Chunky adds more generic level chunks to many of the dungeons and some of the overworld. I recommend using both as they complement each other very well.

## Cosmetics Content

### Original MOD URL:
https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=216767048

### Analysis:  
The file naming is very strange  
My guess is:  
- ARMORLOOK corresponds to the cosmetic outfit  
- ARMORONE corresponds to the first equipment set  
- ARMORTWO corresponds to the second equipment set  
To remove the cosmetic SLOT from the equipment panel, you need to go to \IMBA-MOD-符文觉醒-暗黑传奇\MEDIA\UI\MENUS\INGAMEMENUS\PLAYER_INVENTORY.LAYOUT and delete the CosmeticSlots and related Sections
