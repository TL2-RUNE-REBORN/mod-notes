This 2023 plain-text recipe list has been upgraded to the **Transmutation Recipe Codex**:

**[▶ Open the Recipe Codex](/tools/recipes/)** (UI in Chinese; item names from the mod data)

What the codex adds over this page:

- **Complete**: all 455 recipes extracted directly from the mod DAT files — including
  the socketing recipe and hidden recipes the old list missed;
- **Item icons**: ingredients and results with in-game icons and rarity colors;
- **Probabilities**: random-result recipes (e.g. 50 Lost Shards → 88 artifacts) are
  expanded with per-item probabilities, and gamble recipes that can yield nothing
  (e.g. 1 legendary → 50% shard / 50% nothing) say so explicitly;
- **Searchable**: search by recipe, ingredient or result name; filter by group and
  source component.

The codex is regenerated with each modpack release; see the tool page:
[Challenger Continent · Transmutation Recipe Codex](/tools/recipe-gallery/).
